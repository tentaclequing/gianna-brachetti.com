## How this site is made

This site is built with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) and a custom theme called *signal-noise*. Content is written in [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/) and Markdown. The design was created with AI assistance ([Claude Code](https://claude.ai/claude-code) by Anthropic).

**Typography:** [Cormorant Garamond](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Cormorant+Garamond) for headings, [Fira Code](https://github.com/tonsky/FiraCode) for body text and code.

**Hosting:** [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) with DNS and email through [All-Inkl](https://all-inkl.com/).

**Source of truth:** Local Markdown files, version-controlled with Git.
