Being sick on a long weekend made me browse the web a tad too much but I found helpful things I'm sharing with you so I count that as a success: 

Websites sharing their media kit including alt text you can just copypaste. How's that for accessibility? 
https://notaidoc.com/share

Now this might have been around for a long time without me registering (I rarely ever need to check a company's media kit), but I wish that was a thing for anyone sharing a video people might want to share (eg. a documentary, interview, or a really cool ad). If I were to create videos, for example, I'd offer an alt text in the description.
